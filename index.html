Hello, World!

Hey there! I'm Samyak — a kid from Surat, Gujarat who grew up with big dreams. Born in 2007, I did my schooling at DPS Tapi, and after Class 10, I dove headfirst into JEE preparation. Two years of early mornings, late nights, and relentless effort. I didn't crack IIT. I didn't get a top NIT rank either. But I scored 25,000 in JEE Mains — and landed at IIIT Pune, where my real journey began.

🧠

The JEE Chapter

Two years of intensive preparation taught me discipline and resilience more than any textbook could. The outcome wasn't what I planned — but it led me somewhere equally full of opportunity. Not every path is straight, and that's okay.

🏫

Life at IIIT Pune

My first semester wasn't just about grades. I focused on making real friends, creating memories, learning C/C++, touching DSA, and honestly just figuring out college life. The result? A 9.16 SGPA. Balance is everything.
