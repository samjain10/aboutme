About Me

My Story So Far

From Surat to IIIT Pune — an honest account of effort, setbacks, and finding my path.

👋

Hello, World!

Hey! I'm Samyak — a kid from Surat, Gujarat who grew up with big dreams. Born in 2007, I did my schooling at DPS Tapi, and after Class 10 dove into JEE prep. Two years of early mornings, late nights, relentless grind. Didn't crack IIT. Didn't get a top NIT CS seat. Secured 25,000 in JEE Mains — and landed at IIIT Pune, where I quickly realised the destination matters less than what you do when you arrive.

💪

The JEE Chapter

Two years of prep taught me what no textbook could — discipline under pressure. The rank wasn't what I planned. The work ethic? That stayed forever.

🏫

Life at IIIT Pune

Sem 1 wasn't just grades. Made real friends, learned C/C++, touched DSA, figured out college life — and finished with a 9.16 SGPA. Balance is a skill too.
